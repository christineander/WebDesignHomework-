About The Song

The song Dunno by Mac Miller, released in 2018, captures a poignant and reflective tone that resonates with listeners on a deep, emotional level.


Through its introspective lyrics and soulful melodies, the track explores themes of love, loss, and self-discovery.


While the meaning behind any song can be subjective, Dunno conveys a sense of vulnerability, acceptance, and the complexities of navigating relationships.
