How to Make the Best PB&J

Getting Started

Ingredients

  • Jiff Crunchy Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Raspberry Jam
  • Two Slices of White Bread

Supplies

  • Toaster
  • Knife

Instrustions

Step 1

  • Put two slices of white bread in the toater

Step 2

  • After the toast is done, spread 2 Tbsp of peanut butter on one side.

Step 3

  • Squeeze a light drizzle of honey acroos the top of the peanut butter

Step 4

  • Spread about 1 Tbsp of jam on the reaining slice of toast

Step 5

  • Put both slices together and enjoy!
